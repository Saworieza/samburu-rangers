It takes a community to protect a conservancy. Every donation makes a difference.

Our Rangers would not be able to Protect, Conserve and ensure that every conservancy is able to perform its rescue, rehabilitation and release missions if it weren’t for your generous donations.

Make a One-off or recurring donation

Make a once-off donation, or set up a recurring gift. Classy is our preferred donation platform. They process donations from 200+ countries. Use the form above, or click the button below.

Purchase A full Kit

You can help us purchase equipment and kits for Rangers with a one off or recurring donation. Alternatively, you could directly donate your equipment/kits by sending them to the address on our contact page.

Sponsor A Ranger To World Rangers Congress

You can choose to sponsor a ranger or rangers to the tri-annual World Rangers Congress event held in different places of the world. Kindly contact us for more details on how you can be part of this.
